About Me

Sam Riddle is a computer science student and Teaching Assistant at the University of British Columbia. Avidly programming since the age of fifteen, Sam has proven himself to be a well-versed and passionate software developer. Over his many years behind the keyboard, Sam has accumulated a vast knowledge of Python, GoLang, and C, along with experience with C++, C#, and JavaScript. Sam specializes in designing for lower level languages and hardware, while also exploring higher level languages and their inner workings.

Sam’s interests outside of coding include working with personal computers, following the stock market, and practising Muay Thai Kickboxing. Sam also plays video games competitively, placing in the top 0.1% of players globally in Rocket League. Whether it’s a new project, new concept, or a new virtual opponent, Sam is always looking for new challenges to tackle.
